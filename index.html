Hotels

The Embassy Suites at Dallas Love Field and Holiday Inn Express in Addison are close to family residences and the church. If you prefer staying closer to the reception venue, the Renaissance and Fairmont are two classic downtown Dallas hotels with affordable nightly rates.

We do not recommend staying close to DFW airport due to distance.

Please let us know where you will be staying so we can have a gift for you when you arrive as a thank you for traveling from far and wide!

Transportation

Everything is bigger in Texas, including distances! Thankfully rental car prices are not. You can use Uber or Lyft around town; the light rail and bus are not very convenient.

If you are flying in and not renting a car, please let us know your flight info and we will try to arrange to pick you up from the airport.
